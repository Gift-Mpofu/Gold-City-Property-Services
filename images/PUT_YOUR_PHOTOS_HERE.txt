GOLD CITY PROPERTY SERVICES - IMAGE CHECKLIST
================================================
Drop your photos into this "images" folder using these EXACT filenames.
The website is already coded to look for these names - just rename and drop in.

1.  logo.png                  -> Your logo (PNG with transparent background works best)

2.  hero.jpg                  -> Big banner photo for the top of the page
                                  (wide landscape shot, completed project or team at work)

3.  electrical-before.jpg     -> Electrical job - BEFORE photo
4.  electrical-after.jpg      -> Electrical job - AFTER photo  (same spot/angle as #3 ideally)

5.  painting-before.jpg       -> Painting job - BEFORE photo
6.  painting-after.jpg        -> Painting job - AFTER photo    (same spot/angle as #5 ideally)

7.  construction-before.jpg   -> Construction job - BEFORE photo
8.  construction-after.jpg    -> Construction job - AFTER photo (same spot/angle as #7 ideally)

9.  work-1.jpg                -> Any completed job photo (e.g. electrical panel)
10. work-2.jpg                -> Any completed job photo (e.g. painted room)
11. work-3.jpg                -> Any completed job photo (e.g. outdoor lighting)
12. work-4.jpg                -> Any completed job photo (e.g. wallpaper wall)
13. work-5.jpg                -> Any completed job photo (e.g. wall repair)
14. work-6.jpg                -> Any completed job photo (e.g. LED lighting)

TIPS:
- JPG is fine for photos, PNG is better for the logo (keeps transparency)
- Keep photo file sizes reasonable (under 500KB each) so the site loads fast -
  you can compress them for free at https://squoosh.app before uploading
- For the before/after sliders, try to use photos taken from the same angle/spot
  so the comparison actually lines up visually
- This text file is just instructions - delete it once your photos are all in place

Once all 14 files are in this folder, upload the whole website folder
(index.html + this images folder) to your hosting.
